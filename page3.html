Medical Report

What are the requirement to apply for the Medical Report?

1

If you are the patient:

– Application for Release of Medical Information Form
– A copy of patient’s ID (front and back) or Passport

2

If you are requesting on behalf of a patient :

– Application for Release of Medical Information Form
– Consent for Release of Medical Information (this is to comply with the — Personal Data Protection Act XXX) – to be signed by patient
– A copy of patient’s ID (front and back) or Passport
– A copy of requestor’s ID (front and back) or Passport

3

For Deceased Patient :

– Application for Release of Medical Information Form
– Consent for Release of Medical Information
– Copy of Deceased’s Death Certificate
– A copy of requestor’s ID (front and back) – NRIC or Passport
– Grant of Probate / Letter of Administration

4

Where applicable:

– Official Application Letter – i.e. Insurance Query Letter / Claim Form, Lawyer Letter, SOCSO form, etc.
– Written authorization letter from patient (if collected by other than patient or requestor). 
– Marriage Certificate (for married couple).
